Hey! I'm an R&D engineer specializing in microfluidics with a build-whatever-it-takes mindset. I play at the intersection of engineering and biology. 


You can find me in lab rolling up my sleeves at the bench, cell culture hood, machine shop, cleanroom, and keyboard. 
  
  
Currently at [Calico Life Sciences](https://www.calicolabs.com/), building practical, high-impact systems that solve real biological bottlenecks. My focus is on developing ultra-high-throughput, miniaturized screening workflows. 


The goal isn't just to make the hardware faster or just to build cool tools; it's to generate massive, function-first datasets that fuel and validate AI models. 


You can learn more about my work on my [projects](./projects.html) and [publications](./publications.html) pages. Check out these microfluidic [resources](./resources.html) too.
